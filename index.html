About

I work at the intersection of policy implementation and technology, focusing on regulatory requirements in resource-constrained environments.

My practice combines regulatory technology platform development, policy research on implementation effectiveness, and consulting on sustainability compliance.

Current Work

COMPLY
A regulatory technology platform that maps and simplifies compliance requirements for businesses operating in small island states. COMPLY addresses the density and fragmentation of environmental and sustainability regulations in resource-constrained jurisdictions, providing structured guidance through what would otherwise be an impenetrable thicket of overlapping obligations.
